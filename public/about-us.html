Biography

With a distinguished career spanning over 24 years, Mr. Ramesh Nair is an esteemed Consultant Neurosurgeon celebrated for his exceptional expertise in Neurovascular and Skull Base Surgery. His impressive journey in the realm of neurosurgery reflects a commitment to excellence, backed by comprehensive training in three countries: India, Japan, and the United Kingdom.

Mr. Nair's qualifications are a testament to his pursuit of medical excellence. He holds a Master of Surgery (MS) in General Surgery and Diplomate of National Board (DNB) certifications in both General Surgery and Neurosurgery. Additionally, he earned his Master of Chirurgiae (MCh) in Neurosurgery and pursued specialised fellowship training at prestigious institutions, including Fujita Health University in Nagoya, Japan.

Throughout his illustrious career, Mr. Nair's clinical interests have evolved to encompass a broad spectrum of neurological specialties. From managing intricate cases of Lumbar and Cervical Disc Disease to performing precise Brain Tumour Surgeries, his unwavering dedication to patient well-being is evident.

Mr. Ramesh Nair continues to make a profound impact on the lives of his patients. His surgical prowess and empathetic approach have garnered him recognition and respect from both colleagues and patients, solidifying his reputation as a highly skilled and compassionate neurosurgeon.

In addition to his clinical practice, Mr. Nair actively contributes to the advancement of neurosurgery through his faculty roles at national and international conferences. He shares his expertise and knowledge with the medical community, furthering the field's understanding and benefiting patients worldwide.

As a testament to his dedication and expertise, Mr. Ramesh Nair holds membership in esteemed medical associations, including the British Medical Association (BMA), the British Skull Base Society (BSBS), and the Indian Medical Association (IMA).

Through his tireless efforts, Mr. Nair continues to redefine the boundaries of neurosurgical care. His compassionate approach, coupled with his relentless pursuit of medical advancements, has not only transformed countless lives but also solidified his place as a respected leader in the field of neurosurgery.

Areas of Interest

Dr. Ramesh Nair is an actively engaged neurosurgeon, known for his involvement in cutting-edge clinical research trials. His work has been featured in esteemed peer-reviewed journals, including the Journal of the State Medical Society and Baylor University Medical Center's Proceedings.

In 2002, Dr. Ramesh Nair pursued a one-year fellowship in Skull Base and Neurovascular Surgery at Fujita Health University, Nagoya, Japan. In the UK, he received training at Oldchurch Hospital, London, and Charing Cross Hospital, London. After completing his formal training, he dedicated an additional 21 months to specialise in Skull Base and Neurovascular Surgery at Charing Cross Hospital. Before assuming his current role as a senior consultant neurosurgeon, he served as a locum consultant Neurosurgeon at John Radcliffe Infirmary, Oxford. Since 2010, he has been an integral part of the esteemed team at Imperial College Healthcare NHS Trust, offering his expertise as a skilled Skull Base and Neurovascular Surgeon.
